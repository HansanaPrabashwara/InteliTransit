IntelliTransit

Smart, Dynamic, and Passenger-Centric Public Transportation

Embark on a new era of commuting. Harnessing the synergy of IoT , we bring you a dynamic and responsive public transportation system, optimizing efficiency, reducing wait times, and putting passengers at the forefront of travel innovation.

Our solution introduces a dynamic and responsive public transportation system. It addresses the challenges of fixed schedules, crowded peak hours, and underutilized non-peak times by autonomously adjusting vehicle speeds based on real-time passenger counts and demand.

In our proposed public transportation solution, IoT (Internet of Things) serves as a foundational technology to enhance the efficiency and responsiveness of the entire system. The IoT devices embedded in each public vehicle, including cameras, sensors, and communication modules, enable real-time data collection and sharing.

The system enhances overall efficiency, reduces wait times, and ensures a responsive and coordinated transportation network, ultimately enhancing the commuting experience.
